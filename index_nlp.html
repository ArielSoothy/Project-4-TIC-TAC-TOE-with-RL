Key Insights

Achieved excellent performance: 98.0% accuracy, 98.0% precision, 96.2% recall on emotion detection
Multi-dataset training (9,631 samples) achieved 93.5% accuracy with improved class balance (81.4% non-neutral)
Cross-domain evaluation: 79.8% on emotions, 47.2% on sentiment, demonstrating domain adaptation challenges
T5-small scored lower than ground truth in all 10 test cases (ROUGE-2: 0.091 vs 0.356)
Semantic search achieved high similarity scores for specific queries (0.67 for Leonardo DiCaprio)
Training efficiency: 5 epochs in 36:57 with excellent loss convergence (0.154→0.004 training loss)

Technical Implementation

Data Processing

  • Tokenization with BERT tokenizer
  • Text preprocessing and cleaning
  • Sequence padding and truncation

Model Architecture

  • DistilBERT-base-multilingual-cased
  • T5-small for summarization
  • Custom classification head

Training Strategy

  • AdamW optimizer with learning rate scheduling
  • Gradient clipping for stability
  • Early stopping to prevent overfitting
